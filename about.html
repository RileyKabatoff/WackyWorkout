Frequently Asked Questions

What is Wacky Workout Tracker?
Wacky Workout Tracker is a simple, user-friendly fitness application that helps you log workouts, set goals, and track your progress over time. It's designed to be fun and motivating without overwhelming you with unnecessary features.
Is Wacky Workout free to use?
Yes! Wacky Workout Tracker is completely free to use. We believe everyone should have access to quality fitness tracking tools.
How do I log a workout?
Simply navigate to the "Log Workout" page, fill in the exercise details including name, sets, reps, duration, and weight, then click "LOG WORKOUT". Your workout will be saved and added to your dashboard.
Can I set fitness goals?
Absolutely! Visit the "Goals" page to create custom fitness goals. You can set target numbers and deadlines, and the app will track your progress automatically as you log workouts.
What does the streak counter mean?
The streak counter shows how many consecutive days you've logged at least one workout. Keep your streak alive by working out daily!
How is my data stored?
Your workout data is stored securely in our database. We take privacy seriously and never share your personal fitness information with third parties.
Can I export my workout data?
Data export functionality is currently in development and will be available in a future update. Stay tuned!
